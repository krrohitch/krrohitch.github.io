Internshala

App Design · Design System

As a UI/UX Design intern at Internshala, I built their design system and redesigned the Android app.

I worked with the UI/UX lead to build an inclusive system that defines identity, consistency and scalability. Based on Brad Frost’s Atomic Design, it is a comprehensive framework, style guide & component library.

Later, I designed the end-to-end experience & high-fidelity prototypes for the Internshala App, based on the new design system. It was focused on clear information, intuitive interactions and a unified experience. Also, I collaborated with engineers for dev hand-offs & user testing.

The redesigned app was launched on Play Store on January 20th.
