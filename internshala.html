Introduction

I interned as UI/UX Designer at Internshala, from October 2019 to February 2020. It is the largest internships & training platform in India with 6 million+ active students & 100 thousand+ companies/startups. With great numbers come great bandwidths.

Right from the start I was actively contributing to the new Internshala design system, building the style guide library comprehensively on Figma. This was the first time a consistent pattern was designed by the team and hence it required multiple UI audits & testing. After 2 months of effective work, we had a progressive system with defined components. At this point, it was decided to start with the product design for Internshala Android app, reflecting the fresh new visual style.

Problem

Information: Redundant and repetitive data increased cognitive load while browsing. Harder to read through byte-sized information quickly.

Complex: Lack of clarity & heirarchy in forms and cards were obstacle to user flow. Intricate iconography at smaller sizes was illegible and hard to understand.

Consistency: The Internshala app wasn’t designed fully in the earlier versions. Few screens were directly coded by developers and weren’t available as prototypes.
